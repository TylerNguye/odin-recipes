Kimbop

Description

A popular Korean dish that is very simple to make and eat at various occasions. It looks similar to sushi but has different fillings which typically do not include raw fish.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Sesame Oil
  • Eggs
  • Dry Seaweed
  • Cucumber
  • Carrot
  • Ham

Steps

  1. Rinse rice in a strainer until water is clear. Combine rice with water and boil until tender. Add sesame oil and salt to cooked rice.
  2. While rice simmers, beat eggs and cook in pan until slightly brown.
  3. Separate dry seaweed into 1/2 inch strips and place rice and desired fillings.
  4. Use a sushi roll mat to create cylindrical shapes and cut into 6 pieces.
