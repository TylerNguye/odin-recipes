Tres Leches

A popular Mexican dish that features three types of milk: whole, evaporated and condensed. It is a not dense sponge cake soaked in liquid.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Unsalted Butter
  • White Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Whole Milk
  • Sweetened Condensed Milk
  • Evaporated Milk
  • Heavy Whipping Cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a baking pan
  2. Sift flour and baking powder together
  3. Beat sugar and butter in a large bowl until light and fluffy. Add eggs and vanilla. Mix well, add flour mixture and mix well again.
  4. Pour batter into prepared pan and bake for about 30 minutes. Pierce cake multiple times with a fork.
  5. Mix whole, condensed and evaporated milks.
  6. Pour the mixture over the cooled cake.
  7. Whip cream, sugar and vanilla to form and apply whipping to the top of the cake.
